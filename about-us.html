About Us
Who We Are

Founded in 2017 in Australia, Dozin Company is a forward-thinking technology enterprise specializing in Software Development, Research & Development (R&D), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Robotics. Since its inception, the company has been dedicated to transforming ideas into intelligent, high-impact solutions that drive innovation and efficiency across industries.

From its early beginnings as a software innovation startup, Dozin Company has evolved into a globally oriented technology partner with a strong focus on developing adaptable and sustainable digital ecosystems. Its R&D initiatives combine advanced data science, automation, and AI-driven analytics to solve complex challenges in an increasingly connected world.

In 2025, Dozin Company entered a new strategic phase under the leadership and ownership of Ahtera and The Ahtera Group. This transition strengthened its position as a pioneer in AI-driven technologies and robotics, opening a new era of research expansion, global collaboration, and technological advancement.

At its core, Dozin Company believes in the synergy between innovation, intelligence, and integrity. The company’s mission is to build technologies that not only solve today’s problems but also anticipate the challenges of tomorrow — empowering industries, enhancing human potential, and shaping a more intelligent future.
