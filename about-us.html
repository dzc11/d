About Us

Dozin Company is a technology, research, and software development firm specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Robotics, and R&D. Based in East London, UK, the company was founded by Edil Ahtera, who also serves as the Chairman of Ahtera Group. As part of Ahtera Group UK, Dozin Company is committed to driving innovation and automation across industries.
