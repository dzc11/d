Privacy Policy

At Dozin Company, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled with the highest level of security and compliance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information when you use our services. Additionally, we gather technical data, including IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns, to improve our platform's performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and enhance our services, process transactions, communicate with you, and ensure compliance with legal requirements. We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

3. Data Protection & Security

Dozin Company implements advanced security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and provide targeted content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you have any concerns regarding data privacy, please contact us at privacy@dozin.company.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in regulations or business practices. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
