Company Profile

Dozin Company is a technology firm specialising in software development, research & development (R&D), and artificial intelligence (AI). Established in 2023 in Delaware, USA, the company operates as part of Ahtera Group, a London-based corporate group.

Founded by Edil Ahtera, who also serves as Founder and Chairman of Ahtera Group, Dozin Company is positioned at the forefront of technological innovation. The company focuses on developing advanced software solutions, AI-driven systems, and next-generation technologies, supporting businesses in navigating the evolving digital landscape.

With a strong emphasis on R&D, Dozin Company explores new methodologies and emerging technologies to enhance automation, machine learning, and intelligent systems. By integrating expertise from multiple disciplines, the company works to bridge the gap between research and real-world applications, ensuring that its solutions remain adaptable and impactful across various industries.

As part of Ahtera Group, Dozin Company benefits from a global network of expertise, fostering cross-sector collaboration to drive technological advancements and practical innovation in an ever-changing digital world.
