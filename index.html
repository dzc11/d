THE HOEKMEER DISTRICT PROJECT

HOEKMEER DISTRICT is an innovative smart district project based on blockchain technology, integrating innovation, the digital economy, and sustainability. Designed around a distinctive rectangular lake, this district serves as a hub for smart living, crypto-based finance, and Web3 businesses.

The project is being developed in collaboration with Ahtera International (Ahtera Studio for urban planning) and Dozin Company, which is responsible for digitalisation and smart city features.

The Portavera: Smart City of the Future

The Portavera is an innovative smart city project designed by Dozin Company and Ahtera Studio. Combining smart technology with sustainable urban design, this project aims to create a future-forward city that is efficient, eco-friendly, and comfortable to live in. Launched in 2024, The Portavera will set the standard for modern cities, integrating advanced technological solutions for a better quality of life.
